About The Project

ForknPen is a recipe sharing platform which was designed to bridge the gap between home cooks and culinary enthusiasts through a community-driven experience. Unlike traditional recipe websites that offer one-way content consumption, this platform prioritizes social engagement, peer-to-peer interaction, and personalized content curation powered by a robust ASP.NET Core backend.

Key Features

  • Recipe Management: Users can upload and manage comprehensive recipes featuring ingredients, cooking times, step-by-step instructions, and difficulty levels, supported by automatic 10MB image-resizing.
  • Social Networking: A built-in friend system allows users to connect, view each other's profiles, and tailor their experience based on dietary and cooking preferences.
  • Interactive Reviews & Ratings: The community can rate recipes, leave feedback, and utilize the "I Had Made This" feature to upload photos of their own finished dishes.
  • Custom Collections: Users can organize their favorite recipes into personalized, themed collections and seamlessly share them with friends.
