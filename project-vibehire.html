About The Project

VibeHire was designed to modernize traditional hiring practices by providing a centralized, web-based solution. The system offers distinct, role-specific functionalities for both employers and job seekers, streamlining the entire recruitment lifecycle—from job discovery and application management to resume generation and direct communication.

Key Features

  • Role-Based Dashboards: Dedicated workflows and tracking metrics tailored specifically for Employers and Job Seekers.
  • Automated Resume Builder: A built-in tool that allows candidates to input their details and automatically generates professional, standardized PDF resumes using DomPDF.
  • Application Tracking: Prevents duplicate applications and provides job seekers with real-time status updates (Pending, Accepted, Rejected).
  • Community Feed: Fosters professional networking by allowing users to share multimedia posts (images/videos), like, and comment.
  • In-App Messaging: Secure, direct one-to-one chat between candidates and recruiters with real-time read receipts.
